Get Started

Guides You Can Trust

Our guides have a passion for the outdoors and helping others explore it. Know exactly who will be guiding your trip by looking through profiles and choosing the right guide for you.

Everything You Need

Tours are posted directly by the guides that will be running them so you will get all the information you need. Guides post all tour information such as what you need to bring, and even the recommended age for who you should bring.

Message Your Guide

Talk to guides before your adventure even begins. We enable messaging between explorers and guides so you can be sure that who you're trusting is right.

An Adventure For You

Wanderlust is built around connecting people directly to guides who offer amazing tours. We want you to have an experience of a lifetime and thats why we have tours for every type of person.

A Variety Of Destinations

From far away mountain tops to adventures in your own backyard.

On Wanderlust, you'll find a wide variety of tours from far away destinations to tours close by. We offer tours from guides all over the world to help you find exactly what you're looking for.

Bring your family, bring a friend. Whoever you are and whomever you bring, just be ready to have the time of your life.
